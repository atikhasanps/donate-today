Our Donate Plan

Gifts of all kinds are accepted with gratitude, but these are the most
effective ways you can make a difference right now.

Food for a family

Today many families with children, elders and homeless people need our help. For this reason, we are collecting food and other items in order to support the Food Bank of the Municipality of Thessaloniki.

Shelter for a family

In the last year, Shelter House served nearly 2,000 individuals, 60% of which were children. We are always appreciative of donors supporting our cause through in-kind donations.

Education for a child

Education transforms lives and breaks the cycle of poverty that traps so many children. Education for girls is essential — an educated mother will make sure her children go to and stay in school.
